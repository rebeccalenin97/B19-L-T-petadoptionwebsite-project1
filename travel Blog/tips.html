Essential Travel Tips

Packing Smart

Roll your clothes instead of folding to save space. Always pack a change of clothes in your carry-on in case your luggage gets lost.

Money Matters

Notify your bank before traveling abroad. Carry some local currency and have multiple payment options (cash, cards, mobile payments).

Staying Safe

Research local scams at your destination. Keep copies of important documents separate from the originals.

Health Precautions

Pack a basic first-aid kit and any medications you need. Check if you need vaccinations for your destination.

Cultural Awareness

Learn a few basic phrases in the local language. Research local customs and dress codes to respect the culture.
